This recipe is what I call a "very special breakfast" ; just look at the ingredients to see why. It has to be tasted to be belived.

Ingredients

The following ingredients make ine serving:


  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of butter (10g)
  • 1/3 cup of cream (2 3/4 ft ounces)
  • A pinch of salt
  • Freshly milled black pepper
  • 3 fresh chives (chopped)

Instructions

  1. Whisk eggs , cream, and salt in a bowl
  2. Melt the butter in a non-stick pan over a high heat(taking are not to burn the butter)
  3. Pour egg mixture into pan ane wait until it starts setting arounf the edge of the pan(around 20 seconds)
  4. Using a wiiden spatula, bring the mixture into the center as if it were an omelet , and let it cook for another 20 seconds.
  5. Fold contents in again, leave for 20 seconds, adn repeat until the eggs are inly just done.
  6. Grind a light sprinkling of freshly milled pepper over the eggs and blend in some chopped fresh chives.
